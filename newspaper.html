Water to TN dips

24-jun-20004

BANGALORE: The Karnataka government on Wednesday said water release to Tamil Nadu had decreased but not completely stopped, with rain lessening.
Water resources minister Mallikarjun Kharge told reporters here: "Since the inflow is less, outflow has also decreased, but water should flow for the next few days."
On Wednesday, inflow into Kabini stood at 6,840 cusecs while outflow was 6,146 cusecs.
